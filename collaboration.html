What Are ICT Collaboration Tools?

ICT collaboration tools are digital platforms that enable individuals and teams to work together online. They support communication, file sharing, task management, and real-time co-editing.

Core Types

  • Instant Messaging: Slack, Google Chat
  • Video & Voice Conferencing: Zoom, Google Meet, Skype
  • File Sharing & Cloud Storage: Google Drive, OneDrive
  • Document Collaboration: Google Docs, Dropbox Paper
  • Online Whiteboards: Miro, InVision
  • Project & Task Management: Trello, Asana, Jira
  • Enterprise Social Networks: Yammer
  • Shared Calendars & Scheduling: Google Calendar

Why Use Them?

  • Improves productivity and efficiency
  • Supports hybrid and remote work
  • Secure information sharing
  • Centralized workspace access
  • Facilitates teamwork and knowledge sharing

Best Practices

  • Set clear goals & assign roles
  • Communicate openly with active listening
  • Use structured approach: agendas, time management
  • Leverage technology: shared drives, task trackers
  • Resolve conflicts respectfully and early
