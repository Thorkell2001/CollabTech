General Tips for Using ICT Tools

Maximize Efficiency and Productivity

1. Define Your Goal

Know what you want: report, presentation, or research.

2. Pick the Right Tool

Use Word for text, PowerPoint for presentations, Excel for data.

3. Learn the Basics

Explore interface, important buttons, and saving methods.

4. Practice & Experiment

Try features to learn faster.

5. Protect Your Privacy

Use strong passwords, updates, and avoid sharing personal info.

6. Ethics & Responsibility

Respect copyright, avoid plagiarism, manage digital footprint.

7. Efficiency

Use shortcuts, automation, type faster.

8. Collaboration

Communicate clearly, manage versions, use online whiteboards.

9. Future-Proof Skills

Learn programming, cloud tools, data analysis.
