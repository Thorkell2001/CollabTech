Time Management

Manage Your Study, Rest, and Leisure

Set Clear Goals

Define daily, weekly, and semester goals.

Use a Schedule/Planner

Block study, class, and break times using Google Calendar, Notion, or Trello.

Prioritize

Use Eisenhower Matrix: Urgent/Important → Do first, Important/Not urgent → Plan ahead.

Pomodoro Technique

Study 25–50 mins, break 5–10 mins. After 4 cycles, take 20–30 min break.

Avoid Multitasking

Focus on one subject/task for better retention.

Break Big Tasks

Divide tasks into smaller steps to prevent overwhelm.

Peak Productivity Time

Morning → focus-heavy tasks; Afternoon/Evening → light tasks or review.

Balance Study, Rest, and Leisure

Sleep 7–9 hours, schedule downtime to reduce stress.

Avoid Procrastination

Start small, use apps like Forest, Todoist to stay disciplined.

Weekly Review

Check accomplishments, areas to improve, adjust next week’s plan.
