Learn more about CollabTech and its purpose

CollabTech is a student-created ICT study guide developed to support senior high school students in learning productivity software, collaboration tools, and essential digital skills.

The website was designed to present ICT concepts in a simple, organized, and accessible manner to help students perform academic tasks more effectively.

Research Context

CollabTech also serves as a research output that evaluates the effectiveness and acceptability of a student-made digital learning resource based on expert validation and user feedback.
