Desktop Publishing and Layout Design

Purpose of Microsoft Publisher

Publisher allows users to design and create professional-quality publications such as brochures, newsletters, and flyers. It focuses on layout and visual appeal.

Why Layout and Design Matter

A clear and visually appealing layout ensures information is easy to read and attracts the audience's attention. Consistency in style improves overall presentation.
