CollabTech Study Guide

CollabTech is a student-created ICT study guide that supports learning productivity software, collaboration tools, and digital skills commonly used in academic and professional settings.

Purpose of the Website

This website was developed as an educational resource to help senior high school students understand and effectively use various ICT tools. It serves as a centralized guide that explains the basic functions, features, and applications of productivity and collaboration software used in school-related tasks.

The content is organized in a simple and accessible manner to support independent learning, digital literacy, and responsible use of technology.

About the Research Study

This website is part of a research study conducted by ICT students to evaluate the effectiveness and acceptability of a student-made digital guide. The study focuses on how well the website supports learning and understanding of ICT tools among senior high school students.

The findings of the research aim to identify the strengths of the website as a learning tool, as well as areas for improvement, based on expert validation and user feedback.
