CollabTech Study Guide

A centralized ICT learning hub for productivity tools, collaboration platforms, and digital skills.

What is CollabTech?

CollabTech is a student-created educational website designed to help senior high school students understand and effectively use ICT tools commonly applied in academic and professional tasks.

Purpose of the Website

This website serves as both a digital learning resource and a research output. It provides structured explanations of productivity and collaboration tools while supporting independent learning and digital literacy.

Research Background

CollabTech is part of a research study conducted by ICT students to evaluate the effectiveness and acceptability of a student-made digital study guide for senior high school learners.
