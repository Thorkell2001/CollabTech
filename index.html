CollabTech Study Guide

A student-created ICT learning hub for productivity tools, collaboration platforms, and digital skills.

CollabTech is an educational website designed to help senior high school students understand and effectively use commonly applied ICT tools in academic tasks. This site also supports a research study on the effectiveness of student-made digital learning resources.

About This Website

This website serves as both a learning guide and a research output. It explains the basic functions, features, and applications of ICT productivity and collaboration tools used by senior high school students.

The content is structured to support independent learning, digital literacy, and responsible technology use.

About the Research Study

CollabTech is part of a research study conducted by ICT students to evaluate the effectiveness and acceptability of a student-made digital study guide.

The findings of the study aim to identify strengths and areas for improvement based on expert validation and user feedback.
