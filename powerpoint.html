Microsoft PowerPoint

Presentation and Visual Storytelling

Purpose

PowerPoint allows you to create slide-based presentations with text, images, charts, videos, and animations to communicate ideas clearly.

Main Uses

  • Business Presentations: pitches, proposals, reports
  • Education & Training: lectures, workshops, student projects
  • Data Visualization: charts, infographics, SmartArt
  • Interactive Presentations: quizzes, navigation buttons

Key Features

  • Templates & Slide Master for consistent design
  • Animations & Transitions to engage audience
  • Multimedia Integration: images, audio, video
  • Collaborative Editing via OneDrive
  • Presenter View: speaker notes, timers, previews
  • PowerPoint Designer & AI suggestions

Basic Steps to Create a Presentation

  1. Open PowerPoint → choose Blank or a Template.
  2. Use Slide Master to maintain consistent fonts & colors.
  3. Add content: text, images, SmartArt, charts.
  4. Apply transitions & animations.
  5. Add speaker notes for key points.
  6. Collaborate & share via OneDrive or cloud.
  7. Practice using Presenter View before presenting.
